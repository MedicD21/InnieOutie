3. Taps "Export PDF"
4. Tries to upload receipt photo
5. Has been using app for 30+ days (proactive prompt)
6. Taps "Upgrade to Pro" in settings

**Target**: 5% free ‚Üí Pro conversion within 60 days

- Make free tier genuinely useful (current month is valuable)

- Smart defaults
- Minimal taps to value

### 3. NICHE > GENERIC

- "Client" not "vendor"

### 4. LOCAL-FIRST
- Works offline
- No cloud dependency (free tier)
- Fast and private
- Cloud sync is Pro upgrade

## üöÄ Key Differentiators

### vs. Mint / YNAB
- **No bank linking** - simple manual entry
- **Niche-focused** - built for freelancers, not everyone
- **Profit-first** - not budgeting or forecasting
- **Faster** - 5 seconds to value, not 5 minutes

### vs. QuickBooks / FreshBooks
- **Not invoicing software** - just expense/income tracking
- **No learning curve** - instant understanding
- **Mobile-first** - not desktop software
- **Affordable** - $49/year vs $200+/year

### vs. Spreadsheets
- **Automatic calculations** - no formulas needed
- **Beautiful UI** - not intimidating rows/columns
- **Mobile-optimized** - track on the go
- **Categories built-in** - no setup needed
